About the orchid mantis

Philipp Psurek, 2008, commons.wikimedia.org (https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Insect_camouflage_PP08338.png)

The orchid mantis (Hymenopus coronatus) is an insect. The orchid mantis gets its name from its method of camouflage, posing as an orchid.
Its orchid-like appearence also helps it to catch other insects to eat.
